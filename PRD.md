# Waypoint - Product Requirements

## What it is
A tool for created curated, photo-forward map guides. A guide is a styled map plus a set of place cards. Guides are private by default and shareable by public link. 

## Who it's for
A single author who knows a place well and wants to share it - no accounts beyond the author, no social features. 

## Core views
1. Guide list - the author's guides, with cover images
2. Guide editor - a map where the author drops and edits places
3. Guide viewer - the read view, synced map and place cards
4. Public guide - the shareable read-only view at a slug URL
5. Place detail - a place's photos, notes, category, and rating

## Out of scope (v1)
- Following, feeds, comments, saves
- Multi-author guides
- Real-time collaboration
